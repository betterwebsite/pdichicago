Our Signage Services

Comprehensive Chicago Signage & Fabrication

At PDI Chicago, we offer full-service signage solutions tailored to your brand’s vision. From initial design and permit coordination to precision fabrication and expert installation, our skilled team works with metals, plastics, lighting, and custom CNC capabilities to deliver durable, eye-catching signs of any scale.

  • Custom Awnings

    Elevate your storefront with weather-resistant awnings designed for style and durability, crafted to your specifications and branded with your logo.

  • Sign Installation

    Our certified installation crews ensure that every sign—whether indoor or outdoor—is mounted safely, securely, and to code.

  • Custom Metal Fabrication

    Precision-cut and welded metal signs and components, from channel letters to decorative elements, engineered for longevity.

  • Real Estate Signage

    High-visibility yard signs, directional banners, and rider plates—perfect for brokers and agents who need exceptional curb appeal.

  • Printed Graphics

    From banners and posters to window graphics and vehicle wraps, our large-format printing brings vibrant color and crisp detail.

  • Architectural & LED Lighting

    Illuminate your signage with energy-efficient LED solutions or highlight building features with custom architectural lighting design.

  • Banners & Event Signage

    Durable vinyl banners, pop-up displays, and event graphics that grab attention indoors or out, rain or shine.

  • Window Prints & Decals

    Transform glass surfaces with perforated window prints, frosted decals, and large-format graphics that enhance privacy and branding.
